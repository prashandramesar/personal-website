Overview

I have a confession to make: I don't know much about Android app development, so I used gen AI and troubleshooting to help me create an app!

Technologies used: Python, PyTorch, FastAPI, etc.

Project Details

The Problem

I wanted to use my phone to identify dog breeds.

My Approach

I asked claude.ai to generate a barebones Android app which can take pictures, access the gallery and connect to my deployed model.

Results

It took a lot of debugging (Generative AI is at its infancy) and googling. However, for someone that does not know much about Android dev, I managed to do it in a weekend.

What I Learned

You can really increase productivity using Gen AI. Make sure to spend time on troubleshooting errors, prompting and you're golden. I also learned a lot about Android Studio and doing POST Requests through Java.

Future Improvements

The UI needs addressing, the model is trained on images with good lighting and many breeds are missing from the model.
